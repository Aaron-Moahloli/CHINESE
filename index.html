Tones and Pronunciation

Mandarin Chinese has 4 tones. Example: mā, má, mǎ, mà

Basic Phrases

Examples: 你好 (nǐ hǎo - Hello), 谢谢 (xièxiè - Thank you)

Characters and Writing

Chinese characters are logograms. Start with radicals to learn structure.

Grammar and Sentence Structure

Basic structure is Subject + Verb + Object. Example: 我爱你 (wǒ ài nǐ - I love you).
